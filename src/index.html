HTML deserved better.

Sklair is not a framework - it's a compiler that breathes life into HTML,
making it practical, elegant, and expressive again.

An ideological revolt

Sklair exists because we believe the web partially went wrong.
We reject bloated runtimes, synthetic DOMs, and needless complexity.
HTML, CSS, and JS were enough - and still are - if given the right tools.

Open-source.. soon!

Sklair is currently a proprietary internal tool at Numelon.
We plan to open-source it once it's even better, likely sometime in 2026.
We're already satisfied with it - but perfection is our standard.
