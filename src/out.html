The future of Numelon

SinceJune 20, 2025, we entered a new era filled with ambition. Although our website (this one!) is still taking shape, we have not stopped working on the promised projects for a moment.

Rubiš: API Version 3 and a complete app overhaul + native apps

We are currently mainly focusing on rewriting our best product - Rubiš - the file and text delivery platform built for flexibility and performance.
Recently we have been setting up the CDN infrastructure to give you the promised CDN capabilities with Rubiš scraps, and setting up advanced content detection and moderation services to make the platform safer for everyone.

Numelon Passport

Recently, we have also been working on a unified account system and OAuth solution for all Numelon services.
Numelon Passport is designed from the ground up for security and seamlessness.
This project is important in particular because it is also the accounts service that will power Rubiš.

Archiving the Numelon Stock Exchange (NSE)

A few users of our most advanced fictional stock exchange were disappointed to hear that we have archived NSE in favour of reallocating resources towards bigger projects such as Rubiš and Numelon Passport.
We aresorryfor this.

To those who have supported us so far and understood the "lack of activity":thank you.
